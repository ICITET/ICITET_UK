About ICITET 2024

The International Conference on Innovations and Trends in Engineering Technologies (ICITET) 2024 was held from 3rd to 5th June 2024 and served as a premier platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to share their latest research findings and innovations in the field of engineering technologies. The conference aimed to foster collaboration, promote knowledge sharing, and showcase cutting-edge technologies across diverse engineering domains.

Key Features

Invited Lectures

Renowned experts sharing insights on latest technologies

Researchers showcasing their latest findings

Collaboration and knowledge sharing among attendees

Selected papers published in indexed journals

Keynote Speakers

Dr. Rath Satyasambit

Research Technician, RIKEN Center for Brain Science, Japan

Works with neural data from rat's brain using computational methods and dimensionality reduction techniques.

Developed novel bRNN decoders for neural calcium signals during emotional memory tasks.

Holds a PhD in Artificial Intelligence from Tokyo Institute of Technology and Master's in Cognitive Neuroscience from National Central University, Taiwan.

Anusha Nerella

Senior Software Engineer

Anusha Nerella is a forward-thinking software engineer who has delivered enterprise-grade solutions across government and global financial institutions. She began her career at the U.S. Patent and Trademark Office, contributing to large-scale modernization initiatives that improved the scalability and reliability of legacy systems.

She later held key engineering roles at Citibank and Barclays, driving application modernization and integrating machine learning into legacy platforms as part of large-scale digital transformation efforts. Her work spanned cloud-native architectures, real-time data platforms, automation, and analytics in highly regulated environments.

She is currently working with a major global fintech firm, focusing on institutional trading systems, advanced market data infrastructure, and intelligent automation for high-performance financial platforms.
