ICITET 2024 


About ICITET 2024
The International Conference on Innovations and Trends in Engineering Technologies (ICITET) 2024 was held from 3rd to 5th June 2024 and served as a premier platform for researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to share their latest research findings and innovations in the field of engineering technologies. The conference aimed to foster collaboration, promote knowledge sharing, and showcase cutting-edge technologies across diverse engineering domains.
Key Features
Invited Lectures
Renowned experts sharing insights on latest technologies
Paper Presentations
Researchers showcasing their latest findings
Networking Opportunities
Collaboration and knowledge sharing among attendees
Publication Opportunities
Selected papers published in indexed journals
